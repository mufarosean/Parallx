## Collision Coverage

Collision coverage pays for damage to your vehicle from a collision with another car or object. The deductible for this policy period is **$750**. The insurer pays the remainder up to ACV after the deductible.

Covered events:
- collisions with other vehicles
- single-car accidents
- rollovers

Not covered:
- wear and tear
- mechanical failure
- flood damage (see comprehensive section)

## Comprehensive Coverage

Comprehensive coverage covers non-collision damage. Deductible: **$500**.

Covered:
- theft, vandalism
- fire, explosion
- falling objects
- animal collisions
- severe weather (hail, wind, flood)
