## Collision Coverage

Collision coverage pays for damage to your vehicle resulting from a collision with another vehicle or object, regardless of fault. The deductible is **$500 per incident**. After the deductible is met, the insurer covers repairs up to the vehicle's actual cash value (ACV).

### What is covered

- Collision with another vehicle
- Single-vehicle accidents (e.g., hitting a guardrail, tree, or pole)
- Rollover accidents
- Hit-and-run incidents (if collision coverage is elected)

### What is NOT covered

- Mechanical breakdown
- Normal wear and tear
- Damage from flooding (see Comprehensive)
- Intentional damage to your own vehicle

## Comprehensive Coverage

Comprehensive coverage protects against non-collision losses. The deductible is **$250 per incident**.

### Covered perils

- Theft or attempted theft
- Vandalism
- Fire or explosion
- Falling objects (trees, debris)
- Animal strikes (deer, birds)
- Weather events (hail, flooding, windstorm)
- Glass breakage (windshield replacement may have $0 deductible in some states)

## Liability Coverage

Bodily Injury Liability limit: **$250,000 per person / $500,000 per accident**.
Property Damage Liability limit: **$100,000 per accident**.

You are covered when operating your insured vehicle or, with permission, someone else's vehicle. Coverage extends to the named insured and all household members listed on the policy.

## Exclusions

1. Vehicles used for commercial purposes (rideshare, delivery) without commercial endorsement
2. Racing or speed contests
3. War or nuclear hazard
4. Vehicles not listed on the policy
5. Drivers specifically excluded by name on the policy

## Claims Process

To file a claim:
1. Report the incident to your agent within 48 hours
2. Provide a police report if applicable
3. Schedule an inspection with our approved adjuster
4. Obtain repair estimates from authorized shops
5. Submit documentation through the online portal or by mail

Claims are typically processed within 15 business days of complete documentation submission.

## Definitions

- **Actual Cash Value (ACV):** The fair market value of your vehicle at the time of loss, accounting for depreciation.
- **Deductible:** The amount you pay out of pocket before insurance coverage begins.
- **Named Insured:** The person(s) named on the declarations page.
- **Occurrence:** An accident or loss event, including all related damage from a single incident.
