# Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

**Q: What types of insurance do you offer?**
A: We offer auto, homeowners, umbrella/excess liability, and life insurance. Commercial lines are also available through our business division.

**Q: How do I get a quote?**
A: Contact any of our licensed agents (see agent-directory.md) or call 1-800-555-QUOTE. Online quotes are coming soon via our new portal.

**Q: Can I bundle my auto and homeowners insurance?**
A: Yes! Bundling saves an average of 15% on your combined premiums. Ask your agent about our multi-policy discount.

## Auto Insurance

**Q: What are the state minimum coverage requirements?**
A: The state requires $25,000/$50,000 bodily injury and $25,000 property damage liability. However, we strongly recommend higher limits — most of our policies carry $100,000 per person liability or higher.

**Q: What is my liability coverage limit?**
A: Standard policies include $100,000 per person bodily injury liability. Check your declarations page for your specific limits.

**Q: How much is my collision deductible?**
A: The standard collision deductible is $500. You may have elected a different amount — check your declarations page.

**Q: What happens if my car is totaled?**
A: We pay the actual cash value (ACV) of your vehicle minus your deductible. If you owe more than the ACV, gap insurance covers the difference (if purchased).

**Q: Do you cover rental cars while mine is being repaired?**
A: If you have rental reimbursement coverage, yes. The standard limit is $40/day for up to 30 days ($1,200 maximum per claim).

**Q: Am I covered if someone else drives my car?**
A: Generally yes, if they have your permission. However, the coverage follows the car, so your policy limits and deductibles apply.

## Homeowners Insurance

**Q: Does my homeowners policy cover flooding?**
A: No. Flood damage requires a separate policy through the National Flood Insurance Program (NFIP) or a private flood insurer.

**Q: What's the difference between replacement cost and ACV?**
A: Replacement cost pays to rebuild or replace at current prices. ACV pays current value minus depreciation. Replacement cost is more expensive but provides better protection.

**Q: Are my belongings covered if they're stolen from my car?**
A: Yes, under your homeowners policy's personal property coverage (Coverage C), not your auto policy. Your homeowners deductible applies.

## Claims

**Q: How long does a claim take to process?**
A: Most auto claims are resolved within 15–20 business days. Complex claims or those involving injuries may take longer.

**Q: What should I do immediately after an accident?**
A: Ensure everyone's safety, call 911 if needed, exchange information with other drivers, take photos, and call your agent within 48 hours. See claims/how-to-file.md for detailed steps.

**Q: Can I choose my own repair shop?**
A: Yes, but using our approved network shops (see contacts/vendors-and-shops.md) ensures guaranteed work and may speed up the claims process.

**Q: What if I disagree with the settlement amount?**
A: You can request a re-inspection, get an independent appraisal, or invoke the formal appraisal process outlined in your policy and state regulations.

## Billing

**Q: What payment options are available?**
A: Annual (full pay), semi-annual, quarterly, or monthly. Annual payment receives a 3% discount.

**Q: What happens if I miss a payment?**
A: You have a 10-day grace period. After that, a late fee of $15 applies. If payment is not received within 30 days, coverage may be cancelled for non-payment.

**Q: Can I change my payment plan mid-term?**
A: Yes, contact your agent or call our billing department at 1-800-555-BILL.

## Umbrella Insurance

**Q: Do I need umbrella insurance?**
A: If your assets exceed your underlying policy limits, an umbrella policy provides important protection. At $212/year for $1 million in coverage, it's one of the best values in insurance.

**Q: How much umbrella coverage should I carry?**
A: A common guideline is to carry umbrella coverage equal to your total net worth. Your agent can help determine the right amount.
