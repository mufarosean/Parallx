# how to file a claim — my notes

ok so the process is basically:

1. call the agent and tell them what happened
2. they assign an adjuster
3. get your car fixed

that's pretty much it. don't overthink it.

some tips from experience:
- take photos with your phone BEFORE you move anything
- don't say "it was my fault" even if it was
- the 48 hour thing is more of a guideline, I've seen claims accepted after a week
- if you don't like the adjuster's estimate, push back — they lowball the first time
- Mike's Auto Body is the best shop, ask for the insurance rate

also I heard you can file online now but I haven't tried it. the portal is probably at that insurance-example website or something.

Jenny in claims is the best person to talk to — she actually follows up. Avoid calling on Mondays, that's when they're slammed from weekend accidents.
