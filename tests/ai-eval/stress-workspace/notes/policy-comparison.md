# Policy Comparison — 2023 vs 2024

Here's what changed between the two policy years. I put this together for the team meeting but didn't finish verifying everything.

## Key Differences

| Item | 2023 | 2024 | Change |
|------|------|------|--------|
| Collision deductible | $750 | $500 | Decreased ↓ |
| Comprehensive deductible | $500 | $250 | Decreased ↓ |
| BI Liability (per person) | $100,000 | $250,000 | Increased ↑ |
| BI Liability (per accident) | $300,000 | $500,000 | Increased ↑ |
| PD Liability | $50,000 | $100,000 | Increased ↑ |
| Uninsured Motorist | $100,000 | $250,000 | Increased ↑ |
| Medical Payments | $5,000 | $10,000 | Increased ↑ |
| Annual Premium | $1,523 | $1,847 | +$324 |

## Notes

The premium went up about 21% but coverage improved significantly across the board. The lower deductibles alone probably justify the increase for most people.

I think the liability limits went from $100K to $250K per person but I should double-check that against the actual policy docs. [NOTE TO SELF: verify this]
