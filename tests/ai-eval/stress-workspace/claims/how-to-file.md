# How to File an Insurance Claim

Follow these five steps to file a claim properly and ensure timely processing.

---

## Step 1: Document the Incident

Immediately after an incident:
- Take photos of all damage (vehicle, property, injuries)
- Write down what happened while it's fresh
- Get contact information from all parties involved
- Note the date, time, and location
- Get names and badge numbers of responding officers

## Step 2: File a Police Report

For any accident involving:
- Injuries
- Property damage over $1,000
- Hit-and-run
- Disputed fault

File a report with local law enforcement within 24 hours.

## Step 3: Notify Your Insurance Agent

Call your agent within **48 hours** of the incident. Have ready:
- Your policy number
- Date and time of incident
- Police report number
- Names and contact information of other parties
- Description of what happened
- Photos and documentation

**Agency hotline:** 1-800-555-CLAIM (available 24/7)

## Step 4: Work with the Adjuster

After notification:
1. An adjuster will be assigned within 2 business days
2. Schedule a vehicle/property inspection
3. Get at least two repair estimates from approved shops
4. Do NOT authorize repairs until the adjuster approves

**Approved repair network:** See `vendors-and-shops.md` in the contacts folder.

## Step 5: Submit Final Documentation

Through the online portal (portal.insurance-example.com) or by mail:
- Completed claim form
- All photos and documentation
- Repair estimates
- Police report copy
- Medical bills (if applicable)
- Lost wage documentation (if applicable)

---

## Timeline

| Stage                | Expected Duration |
|---------------------|-------------------|
| Adjuster assignment | 1–2 business days |
| Inspection          | 3–5 business days |
| Estimate approval   | 2–3 business days |
| Payment issued      | 5–10 business days after approval |

**Total estimated time:** 15–20 business days from initial report to payment.

## Important Notes

- Failure to report within 48 hours may delay your claim
- Do not admit fault at the scene
- Keep copies of ALL documentation
- If injuries are involved, seek medical attention first
