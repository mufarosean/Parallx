CLAIM FILE — Johnson, Robert T.
Claim Number: CLM-2019-04482
Date of Loss: March 15, 2019
Type: Auto Collision

Robert Johnson was traveling northbound on Highway 101 near Exit 42 when a vehicle driven by Maria Santos ran a red light at the intersection of Highway 101 and Oak Street. Johnson's 2017 Honda Accord was struck on the driver's side. Santos was cited for failure to obey traffic signal.

Johnson reported neck pain and was transported to Regional Medical Center. He was diagnosed with cervical strain and released the same day. Follow-up treatment included 8 physical therapy sessions over 6 weeks.

Vehicle damage was assessed at $8,200. The Accord's ACV at the time was approximately $16,500, so the vehicle was repaired rather than totaled. Johnson's collision deductible was $500 (now $750 under the 2023 policy). Repairs were completed at Mike's Auto Body on Elm Street.

Medical expenses totaled $3,450. Lost wages claim of $2,100 (7 days missed work at $300/day). Pain and suffering was calculated at 2x medical ($6,900).

Total settlement: $8,200 (vehicle) + $3,450 (medical) + $2,100 (lost wages) + $6,900 (pain/suffering) = $20,650, less $500 deductible = $20,150 net to insured.

Subrogation was pursued against Santos' insurer (National General). Full recovery achieved. Johnson's $500 deductible was refunded on June 30, 2019.

File closed July 15, 2019. No appeal filed.
