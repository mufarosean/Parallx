# What To Do If You're In An Accident — Quick Reference Card

**Print this. Keep it in your glove box.**

---

## AT THE SCENE (First 15 minutes)

1. **STOP.** Pull over safely. Turn on hazard lights.
2. **CHECK** if anyone is injured. Call **911** if needed.
3. **CALL POLICE** even for minor accidents. Get the report number.
4. **EXCHANGE INFO** with the other driver:
   - Name, phone, insurance company, policy number
   - Driver's license number, plate number
5. **TAKE PHOTOS** of everything:
   - All damage to both vehicles
   - The full scene (road, signs, traffic)
   - License plates
   - Injuries (if visible)
   - Road conditions
6. **GET WITNESSES** — names and phone numbers
7. **DO NOT ADMIT FAULT** — even if you think it's your fault
