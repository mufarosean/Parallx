## Coverage Types

### Collision Coverage
Covers damage to your vehicle resulting from a collision with another vehicle or object, regardless of fault.

- **Coverage Limit:** $50,000 per occurrence
- **Deductible:** $500
- **Rental reimbursement:** Up to $40/day for 30 days while your vehicle is being repaired

### Comprehensive Coverage
Covers damage to your vehicle from non-collision events: theft, vandalism, natural disasters, falling objects, animal strikes.

- **Coverage Limit:** $50,000 per occurrence
- **Deductible:** $250

### Liability Coverage
Covers bodily injury and property damage you cause to others in an accident.

- **Bodily Injury Liability:** $100,000 per person / $300,000 per accident
- **Property Damage Liability:** $50,000 per accident

### Uninsured / Underinsured Motorist (UM/UIM)
Covers you if the at-fault driver has no insurance or insufficient insurance.

- **UM Bodily Injury:** $100,000 per person / $300,000 per accident
- **UM Property Damage:** $25,000 per accident
- **UIM Coverage:** Matches your liability limits

**Important:** If the other driver is uninsured, you must file a police report within 24 hours to activate UM coverage. The claims process for uninsured motorist claims differs from standard collision claims — see the Claims Guide for the separate UM procedure.

### Medical Payments (MedPay)
Covers medical expenses for you and your passengers, regardless of fault.

- **Limit:** $10,000 per person

### Roadside Assistance
- Towing: up to 50 miles
- Lockout service
- Flat tire change
- Battery jump-start
- Fuel delivery (up to 2 gallons)

## Exclusions

This policy does NOT cover:
1. Damage from racing or speed contests
2. Intentional damage
3. Wear and tear, mechanical breakdown
4. Damage while used for ride-sharing (Uber/Lyft) without commercial endorsement
5. Vehicles not listed on the policy
